Echoes of a Day

About

A student wakes up in their dorm room and begins the day. They go through a familiar routine of showering, brushing, getting dressed, and grabbing their bag before heading out. The elevator ride down is quiet, the walk across the Highline is calm, and a quick stop at D2 for coffee offers a moment to breathe. Standing there, coffee in hand, the student sees two paths ahead. One leads to class: a day of lectures, notes, and learning. The other leads to the beach: warm sun, ocean breeze, a break from the routine, and a chance to feel free. Now, the student must choose between academic success or failure.
